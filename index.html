Where to register to vote

If you have a driver license number, you can register to vote online at the Oregon Secretary of State.

If you do not have a driver license number or the SOS is unable to verify your number, you can still fill out the registration forms online but you will need to print out the registration form, sign it, and mail it to you county elections office.

If you need to update your registration, you may do so here

Find your polling location/ballot dropbox

Voting in Oregon is done by mail, so there are no polling locations. However, you can drop your ballot off at any number of ballot dropbox locations. The Oregon Drop Box Locator can help you find your nearest location.
